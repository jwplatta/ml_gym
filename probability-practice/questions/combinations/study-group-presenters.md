# Study Group Presenters
## Question
A study group has 9 students. The instructor wants to choose 3 of them to present solutions at the board.

How many different groups of 3 students can be chosen?
## Solution
Because order does not matter, this is a combinations problem.

C(9, 3) = 9! / (3!6!)

C(9, 3) = (9 × 8 × 7) / (3 × 2 × 1) = 84

**Answer:** 84

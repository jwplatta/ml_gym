# Quiz Bowl Officer Assignments
## Question
A quiz bowl team has 6 students available, and the coach wants to assign president, vice president, and treasurer.

How many different ways can the three officer roles be assigned?
## Solution
Because president, vice president, and treasurer are different roles, order matters.

P(6, 3) = 6! / 3!

P(6, 3) = 6 × 5 × 4 = 120

You can also think of it as:
- 6 choices for president
- 5 remaining choices for vice president
- 4 remaining choices for treasurer

Total = 6 × 5 × 4 = 120

**Answer:** 120

# Review Session and Exam Pass Rate
## Question
A university tracks whether students attended review sessions and whether they passed a certification exam.

|                          | Passed (P) | Failed (¬P) | Total |
|--------------------------|------------|-------------|-------|
| Attended Review (R)      | 63         | 27          | 90    |
| No Review (¬R)           | 14         | 16          | 30    |
| **Total**                | **77**     | **43**      | **120** |

Compute P(P | R), the probability a student passed given that they attended the review session.
## Solution
Conditional on R, we only look at the 90 students who attended review.

Among those 90 students, 63 passed.

P(P | R) = 63/90 = 7/10 = 0.70

**Answer:** P(P | R) = 0.70

Interpretation: among students who attended the review session, 70% passed.

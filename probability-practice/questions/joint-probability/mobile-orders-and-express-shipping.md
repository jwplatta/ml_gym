# Mobile Orders and Express Shipping
## Question
A retailer tracks whether an order was placed on mobile and whether express shipping was selected.

|                    | Express (E) | Standard (¬E) | Total |
|--------------------|-------------|---------------|-------|
| Mobile (M)         | 48          | 32            | 80    |
| Desktop (¬M)       | 12          | 28            | 40    |
| **Total**          | **60**      | **60**        | **120** |

Compute:
1. P(M ∩ E)
2. P(¬M ∩ ¬E)
## Solution
There are 120 orders total.

For mobile and express:
P(M ∩ E) = 48/120 = 2/5 = 0.40

For desktop and standard:
P(¬M ∩ ¬E) = 28/120 = 7/30 ≈ 0.233

**Answers:**
- P(M ∩ E) = 0.40
- P(¬M ∩ ¬E) = 7/30 ≈ 0.233
